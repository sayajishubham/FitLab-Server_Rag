MUSCLE GAIN SCIENCE

Muscle gain requires:
1. Progressive resistance training
2. Caloric surplus
3. Adequate protein intake
4. Proper recovery

Caloric Surplus:
250-400 kcal above maintenance is ideal for lean muscle gain.

Protein Intake:
1.6-2.2g per kg bodyweight.

Carbohydrates:
Important for glycogen replenishment and training intensity.

Fat Intake:
Should not drop below 20% of total calories.

Sleep:
7-9 hours per night required for optimal recovery.

Indian Diet Strategy:
- Paneer or chicken daily
- Rice or roti for carb support
- Dal for additional protein
- Nuts for calorie density
