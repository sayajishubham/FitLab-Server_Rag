INDIAN NON-VEGETARIAN PROTEIN SOURCES

High-quality protein sources in Indian non-vegetarian diets include:

Primary Sources:
- Chicken breast
- Eggs
- Fish (Rohu, Pomfret, Salmon)
- Mutton (higher fat content)

Protein Quality:
Animal proteins contain all essential amino acids and are highly bioavailable.

Muscle Gain Strategy:
- 1.6-2.2g protein per kg bodyweight
- Spread across 3-5 meals
- Include post-workout protein within 2 hours

Carbohydrate Sources:
- Rice
- Chapati
- Poha
- Oats

Healthy Fats:
- Mustard oil
- Ghee (controlled amounts)
- Nuts

Hydration:
Adequate water and electrolytes are important for performance.
