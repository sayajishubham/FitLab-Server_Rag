KNEE PAIN TRAINING MODIFICATIONS

Common causes of knee pain:
- Patellar tendonitis
- IT band irritation
- Quadriceps weakness
- Poor squat mechanics

Training Adjustments:
- Reduce deep knee flexion if painful
- Use box squats
- Emphasize hip-dominant exercises
- Strengthen glutes and hamstrings
- Avoid excessive jumping if inflamed

Safe Alternatives:
- Romanian deadlifts
- Hip thrusts
- Step-ups (controlled)
- Leg press with limited range

Programming Notes:
- Moderate loads
- Higher control
- Avoid ballistic plyometrics during acute phase

Recovery:
- Ice during inflammation
- Mobility for hip and ankle
- Gradual progressive loading
