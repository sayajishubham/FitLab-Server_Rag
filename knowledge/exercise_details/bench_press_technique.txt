BENCH PRESS TECHNIQUE

Primary Muscles:
- Pectoralis major
- Triceps
- Anterior deltoid

Setup:
- Feet firmly planted
- Slight arch in upper back
- Shoulder blades retracted

Common Mistakes:
- Elbows flaring excessively
- Bouncing bar off chest
- Lack of scapular control

Programming:
Strength: 3-5 reps
Hypertrophy: 6-12 reps

Injury Prevention:
Avoid excessive volume if shoulder discomfort exists.
