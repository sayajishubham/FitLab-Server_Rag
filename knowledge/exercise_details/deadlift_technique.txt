DEADLIFT TECHNIQUE AND PROGRAMMING

Deadlift is a hip-dominant hinge pattern.

Primary Muscles:
- Gluteus maximus
- Hamstrings
- Erector spinae
- Lats (stabilization)

Setup:
- Bar over midfoot
- Neutral spine
- Engage lats before pulling
- Drive through heels

Common Errors:
- Rounding lower back
- Jerking bar from floor
- Hyperextending at lockout

Programming:
- Strength: 1-5 reps
- Hypertrophy: 6-8 reps
- Adequate rest required

Recovery:
Deadlifts create high systemic fatigue; limit heavy sessions to 1-2 per week.
