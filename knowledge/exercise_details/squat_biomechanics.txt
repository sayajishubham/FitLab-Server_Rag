SQUAT BIOMECHANICS

The squat is a compound knee-dominant movement engaging quadriceps, glutes, hamstrings, and core stabilizers.

Joint Actions:
- Hip flexion and extension
- Knee flexion and extension
- Ankle dorsiflexion

Common Mistakes:
- Knee valgus
- Excessive forward lean
- Heel lifting

Coaching Cues:
- Sit back and down
- Keep chest upright
- Push knees outward
- Maintain neutral spine

Programming Consideration:
- Strength: 3-5 reps
- Hypertrophy: 6-12 reps
- Warm-up thoroughly before heavy loading
