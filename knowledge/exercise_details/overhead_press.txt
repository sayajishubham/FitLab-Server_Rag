OVERHEAD PRESS DETAILS

Primary Muscles:
- Deltoids
- Triceps
- Upper chest
- Core stabilizers

Execution:
- Bar starts at upper chest
- Press vertically
- Avoid excessive lumbar extension

Common Errors:
- Leaning back excessively
- Incomplete lockout
- Poor core bracing

Programming:
Strength: 3-6 reps
Hypertrophy: 6-10 reps

Stability Requirement:
Strong core engagement reduces injury risk.
